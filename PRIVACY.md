# Privacy Policy — Planora: Tasks & Expenses AI

**Last updated: May 9, 2026**

## Overview

Planora ("we", "our", or "us") is a voice-first productivity app that helps you capture tasks and expenses. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email address** — collected when you sign in via Google or Apple. Used solely to identify your account and sync your data across devices.

### User-Generated Content
- **Tasks** — text you enter or speak to create tasks. Stored in your account and never shared.
- **Expenses** — amounts, categories, and notes you enter or speak. Stored in your account and never shared.

### Voice Data
- **Audio recordings** — captured only when you tap the microphone button. Audio is sent to our transcription service (Deepgram), converted to text immediately, and **not stored**. The resulting text (your spoken words) is used only to create your tasks or expenses.

### Receipt Photos
- **Photos** — captured only when you tap the scan button. Images are sent to our document parsing service (Google Document AI), parsed for expense data immediately, and **not stored**. The resulting expense data is saved to your account.

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- Location
- Contacts
- Health or fitness data
- Browsing or search history
- Device identifiers for advertising
- Any data for advertising or tracking purposes

## How We Use Your Data
All data is used exclusively to provide the app's functionality:
- Syncing your tasks and expenses across your devices
- Authenticating your account
- Processing your voice commands and receipt scans

## Data Storage & Security
Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL with Row Level Security). Only you can access your data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

## Third-Party Services
| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---|---|---|
| Google / Apple | Sign-in authentication | Email address |
| Supabase | Database & sync | Tasks, expenses, user ID |
| Deepgram | Voice transcription | Audio (not stored after transcription) |
| Google Document AI | Receipt parsing | Receipt photo (not stored after parsing) |

## Your Rights
You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Deletion is permanent and irreversible.
